ABOUT ME
"I'm Jhyrell P. Tulagan, from Quezon City who finds absolute freedom on two wheels. For me, cycling isn't just a hobby — it's where I clear my head and find my quiet."

There is an unmatched high in leaving the noise of the city behind to chase long rides. Travelling by bike completely changes how you see the world; it turns every open highway into a fresh start and every mountain trail into a challenge worth conquering.

Whenever life gets too heavy or problems start piling up, my bike becomes my therapy. I head outdoors, leave the stress behind, and just focus on the road. The burning in my legs during a brutal climb forces me to stay in the present, and every heavy pedal stroke pushes away the anxiety.

By the time I hit a fast descent, my mind is completely clear. It's a lifestyle built on pure outdoor freedom, raw endurance, and the constant urge to ride out the storm until the horizon clears.
