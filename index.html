About Me

I'm Tonie, a full stack developer from the Philippines. I build web applications end to end — from interfaces to backend systems — and sometimes work with embedded hardware when needed. I like keeping things clear, practical, and easy to maintain.

I usually approach problems by breaking them into smaller parts and building step by step. I prefer simple, readable solutions over over-engineered ones, and I care more about systems that hold up in real use than overly complex design.

I also like working on different kinds of projects, learning as I go, and improving how I build things over time.
