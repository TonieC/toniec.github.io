About Me

I'm Tonie, an 18-year-old programmer from the Philippines currently surviving ICT, which stands for Computer Programming but often feels like a full-time adventure in debugging. I spend my days building web interfaces that don’t make users cry, tinkering with microcontrollers until they do what I want, and creating practical tools that actually solve real problems instead of just looking cool. I like learning new tricks, breaking things to understand how they work, and occasionally pretending that my code knows what it’s doing. If you ever need something designed, coded, or magically made to work, I am your person.
