About me

WhoI Am

I'm Tonie, a full stack developer from the Philippines. I build web applications end to end — from user interfaces to backend systems — and occasionally work with embedded hardware.

I approach problems by breaking them into smaller, manageable parts and building step by step. I prioritize clear, readable solutions over over-engineered ones, focusing on systems that hold up in real-world use.

I enjoy working across different kinds of projects, learning as I go, and continuously improving how I build. I aim to keep everything practical, maintainable, and easy to understand.
